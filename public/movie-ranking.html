Best Movies According to Danielle

Top 3 Best Movies

1. The Fifth Element

A super fun and quirky sci-fi movie, featuring Bruce Willis, who is one of my favorite actors.

2. The Abyss

A suspensful, edge-of-your-seat sci-fi movie that will have you feeling all the emotions.

3. Alien

A terrifying, gory film that you just can`t seem to look away from.
